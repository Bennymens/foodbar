Our Story

At Longdons, we believe snacking should be simple, flavorful, and made from ingredients you can trust. Our plantain chips are crafted from carefully selected ripe and unripe plantains, thinly sliced and lightly seasoned to deliver a crisp bite and an unforgettable taste. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of West Africa, Longdons celebrates culture through quality snacks. We are committed to freshness, sustainability, and authentic flavor — using plantains sourced from local farmers and a production process that preserves both nutrients and taste.

Whether you’re craving a savory crunch or a sweet golden treat, Longdons offers plantain chips that are wholesome, delicious, and proudly made for snack lovers everywhere.
