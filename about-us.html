Our Story

At Longdons, we believe snacking should be simple, flavorful, and made from ingredients you can trust. Our plantain chips are crafted from carefully selected ripe and unripe plantains, thinly sliced and lightly seasoned to deliver a crisp bite and an unforgettable taste. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of West Africa, Longdons celebrates culture through quality snacks. We are committed to freshness, sustainability, and authentic flavor — using plantains sourced from local farmers and a production process that preserves both nutrients and taste.

Whether you’re craving a savory crunch or a sweet golden treat, Longdons offers plantain chips that are wholesome, delicious, and proudly made for snack lovers everywhere.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring authentic, wholesome, and delicious snacks to every table. We are dedicated to preserving the rich flavors of West African cuisine while maintaining the highest standards of quality and freshness. Through sustainable sourcing and careful craftsmanship, we aim to create snacks that not only satisfy cravings but also connect people to the cultural heritage and natural goodness of plantain and groundnut-based treats.

What Makes Longdon Snacks Different

Locally Sourced Ingredients

We partner with local farmers to source the freshest plantains and groundnuts, supporting our community while ensuring premium quality in every batch.

Traditional Recipes

Our snacks are made using time-honored West African recipes passed down through generations, preserving authentic flavors and cultural heritage.

Exceptional Freshness

Every bag of Longdon snacks is crafted with care to preserve nutrients and deliver that perfect crunch, ensuring you get the freshest taste in every bite.

Quality You Can Trust

We maintain strict quality control throughout our production process, using only ingredients you can trust for snacks that are both wholesome and delicious.

Community Focused

Beyond great snacks, we're committed to sustainability and supporting the communities that make our products possible, creating positive impact with every sale.

Made with Passion

Every snack we create is made with passion and dedication to flavor, bringing you the perfect balance of sweet, savory, and spicy in every variety we offer.
